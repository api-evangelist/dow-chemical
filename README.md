# Dow Chemical (dow-chemical)

Dow Chemical, now operating as Dow Inc., is a global materials science company headquartered in Midland, Michigan. Dow develops innovative products and solutions across three segments - Packaging and Specialty Plastics, Industrial Intermediates and Infrastructure, and Performance Materials and Coatings - serving packaging, infrastructure, mobility, and consumer applications.
